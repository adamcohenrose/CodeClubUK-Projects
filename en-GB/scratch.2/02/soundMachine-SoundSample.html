Activity Checklist

  • Create a new sprite and make it resemble the sound you will be making.

  • In the sounds tab, create a new recording or import a sound.

  • When the sprite is clicked, broadcast the same name as your
    sprite.

  • Now we need to play the sound when it receives the
    broadcast.

  • Finally, make the sprite change in appearance when the sound.{blockpurple}
    is played.
